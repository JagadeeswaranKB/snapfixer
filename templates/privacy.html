Privacy Policy

At SnapFixer, we take your privacy extremely seriously. We operate on a "zero-knowledge" basis wherever possible.

1. Image Processing

When you upload a photo to SnapFixer, it is processed either directly in your browser or on our secure, ephemeral servers. We do not permanently store your photos. Once your passport photo is generated and downloaded, the original and processed files are automatically deleted from our system within minutes.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via our contact form). We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.

3. Cookies and Advertising

We use local storage technology to save your preferences (such as your last selected country) to enhance your user experience.

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website (e.g., Google AdSense). These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Specifically, Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

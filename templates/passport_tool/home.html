Common Questions (FAQ)

How can I make a passport photo for free online?

SnapFixer allows you to create passport photos completely free. Just upload a photo from your phone, select your country, and our tool handles the cropping and background removal. You can then download the digital file or a printable sheet.

What are the dimensions for a US Visa photo?

A US Visa photo must be exactly 2x2 inches (51x51 mm). The head height should be between 1 and 1 3/8 inches. Our US Visa Photo tool automatically crops your image to these strict specifications.

Can I change the background of my photo to white?

Yes! Most passport photos require a white or light grey background. Our tool includes an automatic background remover that replaces your background with plain white in seconds.
